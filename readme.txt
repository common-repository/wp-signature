== Description ==

Displays your signature on the post footer. You can select your signature from your media files.

It will give an unique feeling on your visitor so they can memorize and remember that the post is written by you. The signature itself can symbolize a stamp of legal right and a new trend of post of course :-)


== Installation ==


1. Upload the zipped file to yoursite/wp-content/plugins
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
